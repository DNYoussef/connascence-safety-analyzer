## Risk Management & Mitigation

### Identified Risks & Mitigation Strategies

#### Technical Risks
**Risk**: Integration complexity with existing tools  
**Probability**: Medium | **Impact**: Medium  
**Mitigation**: Dedicated solution architect, extensive testing, phased rollout

**Risk**: Performance impact on CI/CD pipelines  
**Probability**: Low | **Impact**: High  
**Mitigation**: Performance testing, optimization, parallel processing

#### Organizational Risks
**Risk**: Team resistance to new processes  
**Probability**: Medium | **Impact**: Medium  
**Mitigation**: Change management program, training, champions network

**Risk**: Insufficient time allocation for training  
**Probability**: High | **Impact**: Medium  
**Mitigation**: Executive sponsorship, protected training time, flexible scheduling

#### Business Risks
**Risk**: ROI realization delays  
**Probability**: Low | **Impact**: High  
**Mitigation**: Phased value delivery, early wins focus, progress tracking

**Risk**: Budget overruns  
**Probability**: Low | **Impact**: Medium  
**Mitigation**: Fixed-price implementation, clear scope definition, change control

### Contingency Planning
- **Timeline Delays**: 2-week buffer built into each phase
- **Resource Conflicts**: Backup team members identified
- **Technical Issues**: Direct escalation to engineering team
- **Business Priority Changes**: Agile adjustment methodology
